Interior Design

Victorian interior decor was characterized by luxury and comfort simultaneously.Dark colors like olive green, burgundy, and gold were common on walls, curtains, and furniture.Luxurious fabrics such as silk and velvet were used for sofas, cushions, and drapes, creating a sense of wealth and warmth.Carefully carved wooden furniture, ornate mirrors, and wooden or richly carpeted floors were also essential elements.The aim of Victorian décor was to create a warm, royal atmosphere that reflected refined taste and attention to fine detai.
