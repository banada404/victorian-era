Art & Culture

The Victorian era witnessed a great flourishing of literature, music, and visual arts.
Arts and culture were an essential part of daily life, reflecting social identity and traditional values.

literature

Notable authors included Charles Dickens and Thomas Hardy, who wrote novels depicting the social and political life of the time.

visual arts

In the visual arts, oil paintings, illustrations, and intricate engravings adorned palaces and magazines.

Music and theater

Classical music and theater also flourished, making Victorian culture rich, diverse, and reflective of refined societal taste.

Victorian Customs & Celebrations

During the Victorian era, social life was strictly organized, and celebrations reflected social class and refined taste.Prominent celebrations included evening parties,
tea parties, official holidays, and family gatherings.Clothing, fashion, and hospitality rituals were an essential part of these events,
reflecting attention to detail and luxury in daily life.
